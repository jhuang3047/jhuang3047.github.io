Before After Reflection

My goal was to create a website for the store I currently work at. I am not completely satisfied with some elements of my final work but I am pretty happy with how it came out. I did not expect to spend as much time as I did and I feel like I could continue working on the organization of my code and naming classes & ids. I feel that if someone else were to look at it they would think it is pretty chaotic. But, I feel that I was able to incorporate many interactive elements which I am pretty satisfied with.
